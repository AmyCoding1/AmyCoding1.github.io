\ Our Services .

At Student’s Life, we help students make Good Things happen. From great deals that save you a lot of future headaches, to finding a temporary home you will be able to enjoy. It’s all our responsibility to make sure getting a Good Service is always simple, easy, and enjoyable, so we can build a culture where people share more daily. We’re committed to building a new lifestyle/culture in Albania. We believe it’s good for the business, for society and for youth particularly.

\ Our Story .

*We have always had community at its heart.* Founded in 2021 by two friends, their aim was to build a community where people like them could find everything they needed to settle into a new city: a job/internship, a place to live and some fun around town. We are two students who aspire to create a better student’s life for the next generation and to provide them with the needed tools which will make their life easier. What we created is the first online Dedicated to Students Platform in Albania– a space where students would find everything needed for the ideal university life.

  • Internships
  • Accommodation
  • Activities and Fun

We’re all about making connections which is why you’ll find that we’re a team that values collaboration, an open, inclusive and honest culture and a team that believes in supporting each other and our communities.
